Berkeley Business Society

Berkeley Business Society is a student-run strategy consulting group that provides actionable sol utions for industry-leading clients in various distinct business concentrations. I consulted for TED conferences and Tempus through the club. As the tech chair, I maintain the website and have also built an algorithm to solve the unbalanced assignment problem (optimize division assignments using member preference points for each division).

TED Conferences

My team and I used social media to develop a dedicated Generation Z community that will consume TED content consistently. We performed market research to identify issues in brand image, promotion, and presentation of content. To combat these issues, we redesigned the TED app, website, YouTube channel, and Instagram profile to improve user interface experience and to increase site traffic. TED implemented many of these changes in Fall 2019.

Tempus

The project scope includes international expansion, cancer and healthcare data analysis, and go-to-market strategy for Tempus. My team and I analyzed healthcare, economic, pharmaceutical, regulatory trends across 8 countries and determined which factors are most important in order to propose viable destinations for international expansion.

Adidas

I designed a mobile application to bolster Adidas’ presence in the eSports market. The app keeps track of a player’s game relevant statistics, send reminders to prevent eyestrain and carpal tunnel, and administers hearing tests to test for hearing loss.
